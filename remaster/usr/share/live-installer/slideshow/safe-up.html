Security and update packs

    Remaster uses update packs.
    With these update packs we increase stability and security by testing the incoming packages before they are released.

  • The security repository is synchronized daily.
  • On the first of each month, the repositories are synchronized and tested.
  • The release of an update pack will be postponed until the next month when critical breakages cannot be solved.
  • Status updates are communicated by blog, RSS feed, our forum, Facebook and Twitter.
  • You can find a complete description of the update pack process on the Remaster forum.
